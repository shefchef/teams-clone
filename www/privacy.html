Privacy Policy

We care about your privacy and only retrieve data that is needed by Microsoft Teams.

This data includes mainly: RTCICECandidates & RTCSessionDescription which is crucial for WebRTC based video calling. RTCICECandidates, RTCSessionDescription is on the server only until the peers are joined. As soon as the call it cut the data is removed.

WebRTC's RTCDataChannel interface is used for the video call RTCDataChannel to transfer data directly from one peer to another.

Microsoft Teams is purely peer-to-peer,and thus any shared data doesn't get saved the server.
